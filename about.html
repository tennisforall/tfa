Our Mission

At Tennis For All, our mission is to make tennis accessible to everyone, regardless of background or resources. We aim to break down barriers by providing free or affordable lessons, donating equipment, and creating inclusive opportunities to foster a lifelong love for the game. By uniting communities through tennis, we inspire connection, confidence, and healthy living.

Our Origins

Tennis For All began with a simple idea: to bring the joy of tennis to those who might not otherwise have the opportunity to play. Founded by passionate high school athletes and coaches, we saw firsthand how tennis can transform lives, teaching discipline, teamwork, and resilience. From local coaching sessions to global outreach efforts, our story is one of growth and community impact.
